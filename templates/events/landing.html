Features

One unified platform for competitions, secure elections, and ticketed events.

Vootely provides organizers with the tools to manage voting campaigns, secure organizational ballots, and sell tickets with fast payouts.

01

Paid competitions

Set vote pricing, publish campaigns, and let supporters vote online or offline via USSD without creating an account.

02

Nominee pages

Present candidates clearly with photos, bios, individual codes, and direct voting checkout flows.

03

Direct ticketing

Configure ticket types, handle online payments via Paystack, and issue ticket links to buyers instantly via SMS and Email.

04

Gate Check-In App

Equip gate staff with our responsive check-in app to authenticate tickets and avoid duplicate entries.

05

Fast organizer payouts

Monitor accumulated net earnings in your dashboard wallet and request fast, secure payouts direct to Mobile Money or bank account.

06

Secure elections

Run structured ballots with credential-based voter access to ensure secure, private internal organizational voting.

Offline Voting

Reach every voter with offline USSD support.

No internet, data, or smartphone required. Vootely provides a lightweight, reliable USSD interface that works on any mobile phone in Ghana, ensuring zero barriers to participation.

Feature Phones Welcome

Works seamlessly on basic phones without needing mobile applications or high-speed data.

Direct MoMo Prompt

Triggers a direct mobile money authorization push request (MTN, Telecel, AirtelTigo) to the voter's screen.

Automated Real-time Sync

USSD votes are automatically processed and sync instantly with your event's online web leaderboard.

Receipt SMS Notification

Voters receive a detailed confirmation text message detailing their transaction as soon as payment settles.
