Paid Event Roadmap

Step 1: Capture Gorgeous Nominee Profiles

First impressions dictate conversion.

Your nominees are the heart of your campaign. Ensure every profile uploaded has:

  • High-Resolution Photos: Clear, professional portrait shots attract more attention.
  • Compelling Biographies: Keep bios brief, inspiring, and focused on why voters should support them.
  • Simple Custom Codes: Assign easily readable codes (e.g., NOM01) so supporters do not make payment mistakes.

Step 2: Balance Voting Prices Wisely

Maximize revenue through volume balance.

A high vote price can discourage voters, while a price too low might reduce overall margins.

  • Ideal Range: GHS 1.00 to GHS 2.50 per vote is the optimal conversion corridor.
  • Offer Multipliers: Encourage supporters to purchase bulk packs (e.g. 50 votes for GHS 50.00) directly in the checkout form to increase transaction values.
  • Zero friction checkout: Supporters pay instantly using Mobile Money (MTN, Telecel, AT) or Cards via our integrated secure Paystack checkout.

Step 3: Dominate WhatsApp & Social Media

Engage supporters where they already are.

Do not rely on voters casually finding your page. Equip your nominees with custom promotional templates!

WhatsApp Broadcast Template:

"Hi family and friends! 🌟 I've been nominated for [Event Name]! Every vote counts, and you can support me directly here: [Nominee Link] (Pay instantly with MoMo or Card). Let's win this together! 🚀"

Step 4: Keep the Leaderboard Live!

Gamification drives exponential returns.

A visible, updating leaderboard triggers intense competition. When candidates see themselves drop in ranks, it sparks massive campaign pushes:

  • Public Visibility: Ensure `Show Leaderboard` is toggled ON under settings so nominees and voters can see real-time shifts.
  • Countdown Pushes: In the final 24 hours of your event, send status screenshots of the leaderboard ranks. Nominees will rally their networks to pull ahead before the window closes!
